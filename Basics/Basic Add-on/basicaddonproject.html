Rock climbing is an exhilarating sport that challenges climbers to ascend routes on natural rock formations in outdoor settings or on artificial resin walls in indoor gyms. Each route is carefully documented in guidebooks and online databases, detailing key information such as the climbing sequence (known as the beta), the first ascent (FA), and the highly sought-after first free ascent (FFA).
